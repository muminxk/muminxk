our story

GYM DINESTY was founded in 2002 as a family owned and operated business specialising in supplying high-quality gym equipment at great prices.

Instead of being just another gym equipment retailer, our founders wanted to be the best in the industry and set their minds to doing so! Over the last two decades GYM DINESTY has grown into one of NYC's largest online fitness equipment retailers, healping thousands of customers live longer, happier and healtheir lives.
